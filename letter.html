写给芳的一封信

老婆：
在这片静谧的星空下，我轻轻落笔，将心底最深的思念写给你。我们的故事，如同遥远的星辰，虽隔万里，却在我心中清晰闪耀，永不褪色。 Beneath this quiet starlit sky, I write to you with all the love and longing in my heart. Our story is like a constellation—distant in space, yet shining clearly in the galaxy of my soul, never fading.
每一个夜晚，我仰望星海，仿佛你也在大洋彼岸凝望着同一片天空。那些来不及诉说的言语，那些深情回望的瞬间，如流星般划过我的脑海，熠熠生辉。 Each night, I gaze into the heavens, feeling as though you too are looking up at the same sky from across the ocean. The words left unspoken, the tenderness in your eyes—all of it dances in my memory like falling stars, bright and beautiful.
愿你在那边安然无恙，愿我们的心始终相连。无论相隔多远，我的爱与思念从未止息。 May you be safe and at peace on the other side of the sea, and may our hearts stay forever intertwined. No matter the distance, my love and yearning for you will never cease.
—— 永远爱你的老公
— Forever loving you, your husband
